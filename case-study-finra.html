SCADpro: FINRA

Client Project

Year

2026

Duration

10 weeks

Overview

In collaboration with FINRA, I worked with a multidisciplinary team to reimagine the digital ecosystem for financial professionals. Our goal was to transform a fragmented landscape of 15+ disparate systems into a unified, role-based experience that prioritizes trust, security, and seamless navigation.

By leveraging a 'Jobs to be Done' framework and deep-dive user research, we moved beyond static personas to design a centralized 'Lobby' concept. This strategic transformation focused on reducing 'mucky' navigation paths and creating a light, professional workspace that meets strict WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility standards.

What I Took Away

Beyond the Persona

Moving from static job titles to a 'Jobs to be Done' framework taught me how to design for intent rather than identity, ensuring the platform scales for diverse professional needs.

Navigating Complexity

Synthesizing 15+ fragmented entry points into a single 'Lobby' required a deep understanding of 'expert bias' and how to simplify high-stakes workflows without losing critical functionality.

Data as a Design Language

Using cluster analysis and quantitative data from 1,500+ investors allowed us to validate our design decisions with hard evidence, bridging the gap between creative vision and business requirements.
